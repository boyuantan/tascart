Welcome to TasCart.

What is TasCart?

It's simple: Community-friendly outsourcing.

Whether young, old, or in-between, TasCart is the solution for you.

Need a favour? Need quick cash? Why not ask a member of your community?

Tascart is a service that connects the various needs of a community to those who can provide the solution. It is geared towards those small, serendipitous, “by-the-way” moments that build towards genuine connections.

What you can do with TasCart:

  • Get groceries delivered to your door.
  • Pick up your delivery from the post office.
  • Get help lifting heavy things.
  • Feed your dog.
  • Set up your barbeque
  • Make friends with your community!

FAQ

Q: How does TasCart work?

A: A user simply posts a request for a small task (e.g. buying groceries), and another user accepts the request.

Q: Why should I accept a request? What's in it for me?

A: The requester can offer a fee. There are also people who are kind enough to work pro bono.

Q: How much does TasCart cost to use?

A: It's 100% free. We do not offer to prioritize tasks on a payment basis as we believe everyone should be allowed to use our service equally.
